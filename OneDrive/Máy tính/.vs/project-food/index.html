Burger is a famous fast food with fragrant golden crust, soft meat and fresh green vegetables. Slices of fatty cheese and rich sauce contribute to the attractiveness of the dish. The perfect blend of flavors creates a delicious, nutritious dish for all diners. Burger is famous for its 4 main fillings including chicken, beef, shrimp and unique crab cakes. The fillings are carefully selected to ensure the freshness, firmness and sweetness of each meat fiber. The rich Burger meat is fried in a medium heat to help preserve the softness of the meat. The crust is baked crispy and spongy with the fragrant aroma of sesame. To add more fiber to the meal, layers of fresh vegetables are neatly arranged under the meat. This helps the crust not to absorb water and still retain the crispy, fragrant texture like it was just baked. Just hearing about it makes you crave it, so let's go eat it right away!
